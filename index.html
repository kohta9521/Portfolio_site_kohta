01.About me

I am currently a first year college student at Gakushuin University in the Law Department. I started studying programming in the winter of my junior year of high school. I am studying and improving my technical skills every day while interacting with interns and engineers at the university.

I also started my own engineering community "ENTER" exclusively for university students, and am managing it while steadily increasing the number of members.


My main area of work is front-end, but recently I have been working on back-end languages and am aiming to become a person who can also work on the server side!

We hope you enjoy your visit to this site!
